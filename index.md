### What the monthly SEO work includes

Every retainer has a simple rhythm: diagnose, build, measure, improve. The first phase removes the obvious blockers. That can include indexation problems, thin service pages, slow templates, weak internal links, missing schema, duplicated titles, broken redirects, local profile gaps and content that answers the wrong buyer question. The aim is to give Google and AI systems a clearer version of what your company does, who it helps and why people should trust it.

The production phase builds or improves the pages that matter. We write service pages, location pages, comparison pages, practical guides and proof pages with real examples, useful explanations and clear calls to action. Each page must stand alone as helpful content. It should tell a buyer what the service is, when it is worth buying, what it includes, what it costs in broad terms, what risks to avoid, what proof to look for and what happens after they enquire.

The improvement phase is where the compounding effect comes from. We watch which pages are gaining impressions, which queries bring qualified visitors, which rankings are stuck, which local searches need more proof, and which AI answers are citing competitors. Then we adjust. Sometimes that means a tighter answer near the top. Sometimes it means stronger internal links. Sometimes it means earning better external mentions. Sometimes it means cutting a page that does not deserve attention.

Our own method keeps the work accountable by pairing a page map, a technical repair list, a local proof plan, an authority plan and a monthly enquiry review. That means the agency conversation stays practical. You know what was built, what changed in Google, what changed in AI answers, what buyers did on the site, and what deserves the next hour of work.

## FAQs

### What is a good SEO agency in the UK?

A good UK SEO agency explains the work before asking for trust. It should show which pages, technical fixes, local signals, content pieces and authority actions are needed, then report progress in plain language. The best fit is usually the agency that connects each task to enquiries, not the one with the loudest award claim.

### How much does a UK SEO agency cost?

UK SEO agency pricing usually depends on competition, page depth, technical debt and how fast you want to move. Small local campaigns can start lower, while competitive national or ecommerce campaigns need more production and authority. We scope the first phase around the work most likely to create enquiries and keep it on a rolling agreement.

### Is SEO worth it for small businesses?

SEO can be worth it for small businesses when buyers already search for the service and the website can convert those visitors. It is weaker when the offer is unproven or the company needs sales this week. We check the market first, then recommend the smallest useful first phase.

### How long does SEO take to work?

Early movement can appear within a few weeks after technical fixes and local improvements. Stronger enquiry growth usually builds over three to six months, while competitive national terms can take longer. The monthly report should show what is improving, what is stuck and what will be done next.

### Can SEO help with AI search visibility?

Yes. AI search visibility grows from crawlable pages, clear answers, strong entities, schema, reviews, trusted mentions and useful content. Those are SEO foundations. We strengthen them so your business is easier for Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude and Perplexity to understand and cite.
